Campus size

 

  
Expectation

  We thought the campus size to be bigger
  It has everything from sports ground to academics.
  A lot of parks and garden inside the campus.
  
Reality

  But after we came in the college on the day
  of reporting we came to know that the campus
  size is too small.There were just 2 hostel
  buildings and one academic building.Rest of the 
  buildings is for JUT students.Also the campus
  can accomodate just first year students only.
  The remaining year students have to stay on
  jupmi campus.The sports side of our college
  is also very dissapointing.The college dosen't
  have its basketball court.the cricket ground
  is also short in size.Overall we have to manage 
  anyway.

Central Mess

 
 
    
Expectation 
 
  We expect that every kind of meal with
  no good quality and taste will be given to us.
  the foods will be not hygenic.we have to order the meal
  online many times to fill our stomach
  
Reality 

  
  The meal given to us is of very good quality.
  It's taste is very nice.Even both veg and non veg is
  given to us which is rarely given in any other college.
  only the snacks part is not much good.Otherwise the meal
  is good in dinner,breakfast and lunch.
  the seating capacity of mess is little bit small.

Sports

 
  
   
  
Expectation
 
  We expected a separate building for indoor games,
  large ground for outdoor games and a gym
  with all those equipment provided to a sportplayer. 
  
Reality
 
  There is a lack of sports field in our college.
  Only badminton and volleyball field is there.
  Rest of the sports field is not well defined.
  Also the college dosen't yet provided any sports equipment.
    
    
 
    
Power supply
 
    
 
    
Expectation
  
             
 We expected a 24/7 power supply and
 super powerful generator thats just starts
 working after a power cut. thereshould be no
 electricity shortage. 
   
Reality
 
 There is a power cut at a regular interval of
 time which creates a lot of problem to us
 specially for studying.Also the generator has
 never operated after a power cut.
 we have to wait for electricity to come. 
    
     
 
      
Internet connection
 
     
 
      
Expectations

 we have expected that we would get a strong wifi
 connection with downloading speed of more than
 1 gbps.Thus, we can download huge games and
 movies in just a second. 
   
Reality
 
 Thers is a jio wifi installed in every floor
 which is shared among the 8 students.We get a
 downloading speed of approx 5 mbps which is 
 very low and it takes a lot of time to download
 any file.
